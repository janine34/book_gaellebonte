The Turning Point

2020
Illustrator - Excel - Html - CSS - Javascript
Turning point is a collection of data and information displayed in a visually appealing journey where we discover the factors and the threats of our sixth mass extinction of species. We decided to design our graphs with Illustrator to create a strong visual identity in black and white. Our website is constantly evolving as we dive deeper in the problem.  Through this journey you can discover and analyse the evolution of our nature and where we are now. Suddenly everything is changing in a very rapid pace yet where is the turning point ? Did we miss it? Are we at the end of the extinction or at the beginning?
